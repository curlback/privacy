1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us and information that is automatically collected when you use the App:

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: Name, email address, username, password, phone number, and profile information
  • User Content: Content you create, upload, or share through the App, including photos, videos, messages, comments, and other user-generated content
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details, and transaction information (processed securely by third-party payment processors)
  • Communications: Messages you send to us, feedback, support requests, and other communications
  • Preferences: Settings, preferences, and other information you choose to provide

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Device type, model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID), device hardware information, mobile network information, and device settings
  • Usage Information: How you interact with the App, features you use, pages you view, time spent in the App, actions you take, and navigation patterns
  • Location Information: Precise or approximate location data (if you grant location permissions), including GPS coordinates, IP address-based location, and location derived from Wi-Fi or cellular network information
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, access times, pages viewed, app crashes, performance data, and other diagnostic information
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, pixels, and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your interactions with the App

1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

  • Google Services: When you use Google Play Services, Google Sign-In, or other Google services integrated with our App, we may receive information from Google in accordance with your Google account settings and Google's Privacy Policy
  • Social Media Platforms: If you connect your social media accounts, we may receive information from those platforms
  • Analytics Providers: We may receive aggregated analytics data from third-party analytics services

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App and its features

  • Personalize your experience and provide customized content and recommendations
  • Communicate with you about your account, the App, updates, and important notices
  • Respond to your inquiries, comments, and support requests
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns, trends, and user behavior to improve the App
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues, security threats, fraud, and abuse
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and protect the rights, property, and safety of users and others
  • Comply with legal obligations, court orders, and government regulations
  • Send you promotional communications, marketing materials, and other information (with your consent, where required by law)
  • Conduct research and analytics to understand user preferences and improve our services
  • Facilitate social features and enable you to connect with other users
