Welcome to the official legal portal for UnistudentsMatch. This document outlines our commitment to user safety, data privacy, and the standards of conduct expected within our community.

Effective Date: March 27, 2026

Part I: Terms of Service

1. Contractual Agreement

By installing, registering for, or using UnistudentsMatch (the "App"), you enter into a legally binding contract with UnistudentsMatch. These terms govern your access to our matchmaking services designed specifically for university students.

2. Mandatory Eligibility Criteria

To maintain a secure environment, UnistudentsMatch enforces strict eligibility requirements. You certify that:

  • You are at least 18 years of age.
  • You are a bona fide undergraduate or postgraduate student currently enrolled in a recognized higher education institution.
  • You have never been prohibited from using social media or dating platforms by any legal authority.
  • You will provide accurate verification data when requested by UnistudentsMatch.

3. CHILD SAFETY STANDARDS & CSAE PROHIBITION

UnistudentsMatch maintains an absolute zero-tolerance policy regarding the safety of minors. In compliance with international standards and Google Play Policy:

  • Strict CSAE Prohibition: UnistudentsMatch strictly prohibits the creation, possession, distribution, or solicitation of Child Sexual Abuse Exploitation (CSAE) or Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM).
  • Proactive Monitoring: We employ automated scanning and manual moderation to detect and remove any content that endangers minors.
  • Immediate Enforcement: Any user found engaging in CSAE-related activities will be permanently banned from UnistudentsMatch without warning. We utilize device-level blocking to prevent re-entry.
  • Law Enforcement Cooperation: We report all confirmed instances of CSAE to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and relevant global law enforcement agencies.
  • Safety Point of Contact: We have designated a Child Safety Officer to oversee these standards. For all child safety concerns, contact: safety@unistudentsmatch.com.
  • In-App Reporting Mechanism: Every profile within UnistudentsMatch contains a "Report" feature. Users are contractually obligated to report any content involving minors or exploitation immediately.

4. Halal Ethics & Community Guidelines

UnistudentsMatch is built on Islamic values of respect and modesty. Prohibited activities include:

  • Solicitation of "haram" or casual sexual encounters.
  • The use of profane, abusive, or demeaning language.
  • Impersonation of university faculty, staff, or other students.
  • Commercial advertising, "sugar dating," or financial fraud.

5. Limitation of Liability

While UnistudentsMatch strives for high-quality matchmaking, we are not responsible for the personal conduct of users offline. Users interact at their own risk and are encouraged to follow our "Safe Dating" guidelines.

Part II: Global Privacy Policy (GDPR Compliant)

UnistudentsMatch operates as the Data Controller for your personal information. We prioritize data minimization and user autonomy.

1. EU & EEA Data Subject Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), users in the EU have specific rights that UnistudentsMatch upholds globally:

  • Right to Erasure: You may request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.
  • Right to Portability: You may request a machine-readable copy of the data you have provided to us.
  • Right to Rectification: You may correct inaccurate or incomplete profile data directly in the App.

2. Information Collection & Legal Basis

We process your data based on Contractual Necessity (providing the service) and Legal Obligation (Safety compliance). This includes:

  • Authentication Data: Email, phone number, and hashed passwords.
  • Profile Attributes: Age, Gender, University, Degree, and religious preferences.
  • Verification Media: Photos used for profile display and identity verification.
  • Technical Metadata: IP addresses and device identifiers used to prevent fraud and CSAE violations.

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

UnistudentsMatch does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Data is only shared with:

  • Cloud infrastructure providers (e.g., Google Cloud/AWS) for app hosting.
  • Safety and moderation tools used to detect prohibited content.
  • Legal authorities, only when presented with a valid warrant or in cases of immediate threat (such as CSAE).

4. Retention Policy

We retain your information only as long as your UnistudentsMatch account is active. Upon deletion, data is removed from our systems within 30 days. Metadata related to banned accounts (e.g., a ban for CSAE) is retained indefinitely on a secure blacklist to maintain platform safety.

5. Security Protocols

We use TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit and AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest. Access to user data is strictly limited to authorized UnistudentsMatch personnel only.
