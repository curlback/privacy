Introduction

Welcome to SipSavvy ("the App", "we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application available on Google Play Store. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By downloading, installing, accessing, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the App.

We collect information that you provide directly to us and information that is automatically collected when you use the App:

1.1 Information You Provide to Us

  • User Preferences: Favorite recipes, saved cocktails, custom notes, and other preferences you set within the App

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Device type, model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID), device hardware information, mobile network information, and device settings
  • Usage Information: How you interact with the App, features you use, recipes you view, search queries, time spent in the App, actions you take, navigation patterns, and which recipes are most popular
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, access times, pages viewed, app crashes, performance data, error logs, and other diagnostic information
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, pixels, and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your interactions with the App

1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

  • Google Services: When you use Google Play Services, Google Sign-In, or other Google services integrated with our App, we may receive information from Google in accordance with your Google account settings and Google's Privacy Policy
  • Analytics Providers: We may receive aggregated analytics data from third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics) to help us understand how the App is used

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App and its features, including recipe content and functionality
  • Personalize your experience by remembering your favorite recipes, preferences, and saved cocktails
  • Analyze usage patterns to understand which recipes are popular and improve our recipe database
  • Respond to your inquiries, comments, and support requests
  • Monitor and analyze usage patterns, trends, and user behavior to improve the App's performance and user experience
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues, security threats, fraud, and abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations, court orders, and government regulations
  • Send you promotional communications, updates about new recipes, and other information (with your consent, where required by law)
  • Conduct research and analytics to understand user preferences and improve our services
  • Enable features such as recipe sharing, favorites, and personalized recommendations
